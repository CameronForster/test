The post Commemorative Gift for RIT appeared first on Red Bridge Engraving and Gifts.

]]> We were commissioned by RIT’s Global initiative to create a gift for their President to present to their partner college in Beijing China. This was a special occasion as the university was celebrating their 120th anniversary, and the Rochester RIT campus wanted to acknowledge such an achievement with a hand made item from Rochester NY.

For the plate, we partnered with Amy Grigg of Amy Grigg Designs to turn a solid piece of cherry to a platter. From there, we were able to use reclaimed solid cherry to make a matching commemorative box. The insert of the box was created by laser cutting and assembling a base that perfectly held the plate and then was covered with thick felt. The front and back of the plate were engraved as well as the top and bottom of the box.
