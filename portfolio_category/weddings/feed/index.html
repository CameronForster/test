The post Glitter & Whiskey Hand Made Sign appeared first on Red Bridge Engraving and Gifts.

]]> This was a unique project for us. The customer, who is a wedding planner, wanted a large yet portable sign she can use at conventions. Her style was modern country/chic. The size requirement for the sign was a bit of a challenge – our machine is only so big, so we had to build the sign in multiple pieces. We used pallet wood for this project, cut down to size, then attached each board with pocket screws. From there, we applied a tea staining technique to antique the wood and give it a rough look. By using the pocket screws, we were able to take the sign apart as needed and fit into our machine. So we were able to separate the sign in 4 separate sections and engrave each separately and then when reattached, have a seamless transition as if the entire sign was engraved in one piece. We were very happy with the results and so was the customer. Glitter & Whiskey Events has since been rebranded as Black Label Events.
